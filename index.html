— About

Every set is
a journey

Luca Shock Pisani is an Italian DJ and producer based in Modena, Italy. With over 15 years of experience across clubs, festivals and live events, his sound moves between house, disco and urban — a selection that is never planned in advance, always inspired by the moment and the energy of the room.

Among his notable performances, he opened for Enrique Iglesias at the Unipol Arena, Bologna (2017), played the after-party for Cesare Cremonini's Stadio Dall'Ara concert, Bologna (2018), and held a four-year residency at Modena's White Night (2015–2018) — one of the city's most iconic annual events. Long-standing resident at Vox Club Nonantola and Baluardo della Cittadella Modena. Currently Saturday resident at Gilda Club, Modena.

More than a DJ, Luca is a curator of emotions. His sets are built around a single idea: that music, at its best, occupies every sense until there is nothing left but the present moment. That rare feeling of being completely here — that is what he plays for.

Alongside his DJ career, he works as a music producer, beatmaker and mixing & mastering engineer under the name Pulpy Beats.
